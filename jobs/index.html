We will hire again in 2020

Depending on market development we plan to expand the team during the 2nd half of 2020. We will then be looking for people with the following profile:

  1. Software engineer
  2. Frontend developer
  3. Engineering manager
  4. Internships in marketing or software development

If you want to join us in our mission to improve security, privacy and usability for cryptocurrencies, send us your application to jobs@shiftcrypto.ch. We offer a dynamic and flexible work environment with competitive compensation.

1.Software engineer

What you bring

  • Ability to write high quality software according to modern programming methodologies
  • Proficiency in Golang or another mainstream backend language (Java, C++, .net)
  • Good understanding of and preferably background in (applied) cryptography
  • Security and privacy mindset, ideally with knowledge about Bitcoin

What we offer

  • Challenging technical problems at the forefront of cryptocurrencies
  • Modern tech stack with lots of opportunities to learn and grow

2.Frontend developer

What you bring

  • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and ideally TypeScript
  • Ability to write clean code with modern frameworks like React
  • Ability to work independently with a getting things done mindset

What we offer

  • Modern tech stack with a lot of freedom to shape the user experience
  • Ability to work part-time with flexible working hours

3.Engineering manager

What you bring

  • Deep understanding of software engineering and IT security
  • Experience in managing projects and leading technical teams
  • Ability to work pragmatically and perform well under pressure
  • Security and privacy mindset, ideally with knowledge about Bitcoin
  • Good understanding of and preferably background in cryptography

What we offer

  • Challenging technical problems at the forefront of cryptocurrencies
  • Growing, international team developing cutting-edge technology

4.Internships in marketing or software development

What you bring

  • Knowledge and passion about cryptocurrencies
  • Independent thinker and problem solver
  • Comfortable in a fast-paced and at times chaotic environment
  • Permissions to work in Switzerland

What we offer

  • 3 to 6 month internship with an opportunity to be hired as a full time employee
