Review a BitBox product

Thank you for your interest in reviewing one of our BitBox products.

Please follow the following process:

  1. Fill out the form below to let us know that you are planning to review one of our products.
  2. Wait for us to confirm that we will reimburse you once the review has been published.
  3. Order your desired product in our shop, pay with a credit card.
  4. Publish the product review.
  5. Contact us at support@shiftcrypto.ch with the subject “Published product review” to let us know that the review has been published, please include a link to your review.
  6. We refund you in full (including shipping costs).
